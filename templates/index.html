Depression Calculator

Depression Calculator(depr-calc) is a depression potential level detection platform. Here you can diagnose how much potential you currently have for depression. This application works using the Learning Vector Quantization method which makes predictions based on data in the form of age, gender, and also characteristics or symptoms that you can enter in the form below.
